Creamy Tuscan Chicken

Description

Creamy chicken dish with tomato and cheese sauce. Perfect for dinner!

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken breasts
  • 1 Jar of sun-dried tomatoes
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 3 cups Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Grated parmesan
  • 1 cup Double cream
  • 1tsp Dijon mustard
  • 3tbsp Oil (from tomato jar)
  • 1 1/2tsp Paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Season chicken with paprika, salt and pepper.
  2. Sear chicken in a pan until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. Using the same pan, heat oil and fry garlic for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add and mix in mustard.
  6. Reduce heat before adding cream. Cook to a gentle simmer.
  7. Add more salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Add spinach and cook until leaves wilt in sauce.
  9. Add parmesan. Continue cooking for 2 minutes.
  10. Add chicken, garnish with parsley, and serve!
