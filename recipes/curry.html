Japanese Potato Curry

Description

Flavourful but light potato curry. Perfect for lunch!

Ingredients

  • 2 Medium carrots, diced
  • 2 Large waxy potatoes
  • 2 Small onions, finely chopped
  • 4 Garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1/2 Celery stick, finely chopped
  • 500ml Beef stock
  • 200ml Coconut milk
  • 4tsp Curry powder
  • 2tbsp Plain flour
  • 1tbsp Dark soy sauce
  • 1tsp Honey
  • 1tbsp Mirin
  • Rapeseed oil for frying

Steps

  1. Heat 2tbsp of rapeseed oil in a pan.
  2. Place onions and celery on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Slightly increase heat, add carrots and potatoes. Fry until starting to brown.
  4. Transfer pan contents to a pot before adding beef stock, soy sauce and mirin.
  5. Bring to boil then reduce to simmer, cook for 20 minutes.
  6. In frying pan, heat 3tbsp oil and fry sliced garlic. Add curry powder and flour. Cook on medium for 2 minutes.
  7. Add coconut milk and honey to pot, bring to boil before turning off heat.
  8. Stir in pan contents to thicken.
  9. Serve with rice and sliced green chilli.
