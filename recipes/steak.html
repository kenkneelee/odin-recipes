Steak Breakfast

Description

A full, hearty breakfast to start your day right!

Ingredients

  • 1 Ribeye steak
  • 2 Eggs
  • 4-5 Pre-packaged hashbrowns
  • 1 Tomato, half sliced (or cherry tomatoes)
  • 4-5 Brown Mushrooms
  • 1/2tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Bake hashbrowns according to package.
  2. Rub steak with oil and salt.
  3. Wash and dry mushrooms and tomatoes.
  4. Set cast iron skillet on high heat (no oil) 3-5 minutes before hashbrowns are ready.
  5. Remove hash browns when done; lower oven to 130c.
  6. Place tomato, mushrooms, and steak in skillet.
  7. Sear both sides of steak for one minute each.
  8. Season vegetables and add olive oil.
  9. Place in oven for 5-7 minutes.
  10. Remove skillet from oven, placing on medium stove heat. Remove steak to rest.
  11. Butter skillet, brush excess onto resting steak.
  12. Crack eggs on skillet, season to taste.
  13. Remove skillet from heat.
  14. Slice and add steak back to skillet.
  15. Serve! No need to plate.
